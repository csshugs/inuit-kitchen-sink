inuit kitchen sink

This kitchen sink is intended to present to you inuitcss and will provide a quick overview of pretty much all of the built-in features, objects and helpers of the framework.

When you seriously consider using inuitcss on a real project level, have a read to learn how to integrate it into your project and get an advanced setup instruction and introduction.
